My Journey to Software Engineering

“Life is a Journey, not a Destination.” –Ralph Waldo Emerson

I started learning computer programming in school but never had the opportunity to go deep into it. Sometime last year, I applied to join the Andela Learning Community to learn how to develop responsive web applications. It was a very exciting and life-changing moment in my career as I connected with a lot of like minds and amazing developers and learnt a lot to level up on my tech skills.

I got to know about Andela at the ALC meet-ups. I was really impressed with what I got to learn about the company and how it advances human potential by powering today’s engineering teams and investing in tomorrow’s leaders. I applied to join Andela and although I did not get in at my first try, the application process itself was an amazing learning journey. In the few weeks I’ve been at Andela, the experience has been nothing but amazing.

The journey ahead will definitely be challenging but I am looking forward to learning from the experience and ultimately growing to become a proficient software engineer working with top engineering teams building world class products.
